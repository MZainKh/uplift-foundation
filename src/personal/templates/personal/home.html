Vision & Mission

A Distinct Identity

Boost youth involvement in social initiatives, let youth take charge and cater to the society The Uplift Foundation calls for restoring, recreating and reinventing the perception of the good in people, molding them into socially aware, responsible and compassionate adults

The Uplift Foundation aims to establish initiatives that benefit People of Determination, Orphans, Senior Citizens and work towards Youth Wellbeing and Awareness. We affirm these initiatives will be catalysts for strengthening the commitment and contribution of youth towards the society.

Who We Are

The Uplift Foundation knows that our strength lies not only in the words we stand by, but most importantly through the actions of our initiatives. Our Non-Profit Organization realized that by working together we could overcome our challenges much more efficiently, and that is why we ultimately decided to launch the Uplift Foundation.
